// ABOUT ME

I am a GIS professional focused on geospatial analysis, remote sensing, geospatial visualization, and data science. With more than 10 years of experience, I have an academic background in Biology and Environmental Management.

While working in environmental consulting, I pursued a master’s degree in GIS in order to expand my competencies and develop the necessary skills and technical expertise that allow me to address real-world challenges by taking a geospatial approach and support data-informed decision-making.

Recently, I have been expanding my skills in Python, SQL and machine learning, driven by a strong interest in the potential of programming and automation for geospatial and remote sensing applications.

This portfolio features a sample of my academic work and personal projects. Due to confidentiality reasons, professional work is not included. Areas of interest include climate resilience, sustainable development, risk assessment, precision agriculture, agroecology, and environmental protection.
